Meet the People

OU AirLab Members

PI (Director)

Golnaz Habibi

Golnaz Habibi is a professor in the School of Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma. She is currently the director and principal investigator of AirLab. Previously, she was a research scientist at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, a robotics engineer at Autel Robotics, and a control engineer at Nargon. Golnaz holds MS and BS degrees in electrical and control engineering from the Toosi University of Technology and Tarbiat Modares University, and she received her Ph.D. in computer science from Rice University. Golnaz is interested in machine learning, robotics, self-driving vehicles, reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, and computer vision.
